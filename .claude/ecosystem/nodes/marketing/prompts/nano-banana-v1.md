## INSTRUCCIONES DE USO

### Cómo abrir una sesión de Nano Banana para ChandeliERP

Al iniciar cada sesión de Gemini, pegar primero este contexto de marca:

```
Estoy creando contenido visual para ChandeliERP, un software ERP artesanal para
fabricantes de velas en Colombia. La marca combina la calidez artesanal de la cerería
con la claridad de un software profesional. El concepto creativo central es
"La Llama que Organiza" — la llama de una vela que también organiza el caos de un negocio.

Paleta de colores oficial:
- Ámbar Profundo: #C17B2B (color principal de marca)
- Medianoche: #1A1A2E (fondo oscuro)
- Crema Cálida: #FAF3E8 (fondo claro)
- Oro: #D4A843 (acentos premium)
- Verde Salvia: #6B7F5E (acentos naturales)

Estética: artesanal-profesional-cálida. Nunca corporativa, nunca tech frío.
Iluminación: lateral suave, 3200-4000K, como luz de vela o ventana de mañana.
Superficies: lino crudo, madera oscura envejecida, papel kraft.
Personaje: mujer colombiana 28-42 años, manos de trabajo, delantal artesanal.
```

Luego usar el prompt específico del activo a generar.

## PROMPT 1 — FLAT LAY HERO (Cover de Carruseles)

**Uso:** Slide de portada para carruseles educativos. Imagen de fondo para superponerle
texto en Canva. Debe ser visualmente impactante pero con espacio para texto.

**Cuándo usar:** Posts 1 (Merma), 3 (5 costos), 5 (Soya vs parafina), 7 (3 señales)

```
Generate a photorealistic overhead flat lay (bird's eye view) on a dark aged oak wood surface.

MAIN SUBJECT: A perfectly finished artisan soy wax candle in a clean amber glass vessel,
placed slightly off-center to the left third. The candle is lit — a small, warm flame
glows at its center.

SURROUNDING ELEMENTS (arranged naturally, not perfectly symmetrical):
- A small dark glass bottle of fragrance oil, cap removed, leaning slightly
- A rough block of raw soy wax with visible texture, crumbled at one edge
- A cotton wick, uncut, coiled loosely nearby
- A worn brass scale with a small paper note showing a handwritten number
- Two or three wax fragments scattered naturally
- A small sprig of dried lavender or rosemary for organic texture

LIGHTING: Warm directional light from the left side, 3400K color temperature.
Creates deep amber highlights on the candle wax and brass scale.
Soft shadows falling to the right, diffused — not harsh.
The lit candle flame creates a secondary warm glow on nearby surfaces.

COLOR PALETTE: Amber (#C17B2B), dark wood browns, cream wax tones, warm gold accents.
No white backgrounds, no cold tones.

COMPOSITION: Leave the upper right third of the image relatively empty with just wood texture
— this is where text will be placed in post-production.

MOOD: Warm, intimate, artisanal. Feels like a craftsperson's workbench, not a photo studio.

TECHNICAL: Photorealistic, shallow depth of field with sharp focus on the candle flame
and main vessel. Slight warm vignette at corners. 4:5 vertical format (1080x1350px equivalent).

AVOID: White or marble backgrounds, corporate styling, perfectly symmetric arrangement,
stock photo aesthetic, overly perfect hands, laptops or office objects, neon colors.
```

## PROMPT 2 — ESCENA ASPIRACIONAL MATUTINA (Post 9: "2 horas más")

**Uso:** Post aspiracional sobre el tiempo devuelto. La imagen debe evocar paz, control
y la libertad de tener un negocio que funciona sin ti.

**Cuándo usar:** Post 9 (2 horas más), Post 6 (Dejar de adivinar), contenido emocional

```
Generate a photorealistic medium shot scene of artisan candle maker's morning ritual.

MAIN SUBJECT: A Colombian woman (28-38 years old, warm brown skin, dark hair loosely tied)
sitting at a rustic wooden work table near a large window. She holds a ceramic mug of coffee
with both hands, eyes slightly lowered — relaxed, content, not posing for camera.

BACKGROUND (soft bokeh, 50% blur):
- Three finished artisan candles arranged neatly on a wooden shelf, labels visible
- A clean workspace with organized supplies: small bottles, brass tools, linen cloth
- Morning light streaming through a window, casting long warm shadows
- A phone face-down on the table (not the focal point — she's NOT looking at it)

FOREGROUND DETAIL:
- The table surface shows: one finished candle, a small notebook (closed), a pen
- Subtle wax marks on the table surface — evidence of craft work done

LIGHTING: Golden hour morning light, 3200K, entering from the right side of frame.
Warm amber and cream tones dominate. Deep, soft shadows create intimacy.
No overhead lighting, no fluorescent, no harsh flash.

COLOR PALETTE: Warm browns, cream (#FAF3E8), amber (#C17B2B) highlights from candle
glass, sage green (#6B7F5E) accents from the woman's linen apron.

MOOD: Peace, control, satisfaction. "My business works, so I can breathe."
The feeling of having reclaimed time. Not staged — real, intimate, documentary-style.

COMPOSITION: Woman occupies the left two-thirds of frame. Right third has breathing room
with window light and blurred candles. Leave upper area for potential text overlay.
4:5 vertical format.

AVOID: Forced smiles, looking at camera, laptops, phones face-up, messy chaos,
overly posed professional-model look, cold or blue tones, white backgrounds.
```

## PROMPT 3 — ANTES/DESPUÉS: CAOS → ORDEN (Post 2: Transformación)

**Uso:** Imagen de impacto para el post de transformación. Representa visualmente el
cambio de "llevar cuentas en cuaderno" a "claridad en un sistema".

**Cuándo usar:** Post 2 (Antes/Después), ads de conversión, stories de testimonial

```
Generate a photorealistic overhead flat lay showing a dramatic visual contrast
between chaos and order, divided diagonally across a dark wood surface.

LEFT SIDE — CHAOS (before ChandeliERP):
- A worn spiral notebook opened to a page filled with crossed-out numbers, erased calculations,
  arrows pointing everywhere, and illegible handwriting
- A calculator with some keys visibly worn
- Multiple torn paper scraps with partial calculations
- A pen with the cap lost, ink-stained fingertip marks on papers
- Scattered wax fragments, a price tag left blank
- Slight feeling of overwhelm — too many items, slightly overlapping

RIGHT SIDE — ORDER (after ChandeliERP):
- A modern smartphone lying flat, screen showing a clean minimal dashboard with:
  the number "$3.780" prominently displayed in warm gold, labeled "Costo unitario"
  and green indicators showing profit margin
- The same candle — now finished, labeled, and priced correctly in the upper right
- A single pen, capped, resting horizontally
- Clean, organized, spacious

DIVIDING ELEMENT: A thin amber line (#C17B2B) running diagonally from top-left to
bottom-right, separating the two zones. Not aggressive — elegant, like candlelight.

LIGHTING: The chaos side has slightly cooler, flatter light.
The order side has warmer, more dramatic lighting with amber highlights.
This subtle shift reinforces the emotional narrative.

COLOR PALETTE: Chaos side: muted grays and paper whites. Order side: ambers, creams,
warm gold — the ChandeliERP palette dominates the "after" zone.

MOOD: Recognition, relief, transformation. The emotional moment of clarity.

TECHNICAL: Sharp focus throughout (no bokeh — need both sides legible).
4:5 vertical format. Overhead view, slightly angled (80 degrees, not full 90).

AVOID: Showing the ChandeliERP logo or any specific brand UI (this is a concept image).
No laptops, no desktop computers, no office chairs visible.
```

```
Generate a photorealistic scene of a Colombian artisan candle maker at an outdoor

wearing a natural linen apron over casual clothes (cream tones). She is arranging

not at the camera. Expression: focused, proud, satisfied.

MARKET STAND:

BACKGROUND (bokeh, atmospheric):
- Busy but calm craft market scene: other stands with plants, ceramics, textiles
- Warm morning sunlight filtering through canvas tent covers

MOOD: Pride of craft, community, authenticity, small business success.
```

## PROMPT 5 — DETAIL SHOT: MANOS CON CERA (Posts educativos)

**Uso:** Imagen de detalle para posts sobre el proceso de fabricación. El "elemento
humano" que conecta el contenido educativo con la realidad artesanal.

**Cuándo usar:** Posts sobre merma, costos de producción, proceso de fabricación

```
Generate a photorealistic close-up detail shot of a candle maker's hands
during the wax pouring process.

MAIN SUBJECT: Colombian woman's hands (warm brown skin, short natural nails,
no nail polish, a simple thin silver ring on one finger). She is carefully pouring
liquid soy wax from a small brass pitcher into a glass candle vessel.
The wax pours in a thin, controlled stream — visible texture of the liquid wax.

DETAILS TO INCLUDE:
- The glass vessel sitting on a dark wooden surface, partially filled with cooling wax
- The natural texture of the cooling wax surface in the vessel (slight ripple, slight matte finish)
- A thin wisp of fragrant steam rising from the just-poured wax
- A small measuring cup nearby with slight wax residue
- The worn fabric of a linen apron visible at the edge of frame
- Slight amber stain marks on the edge of the brass pitcher — evidence of use

LIGHTING: Warm side light from the left, 3400K, creating beautiful shadow play
on the stream of flowing wax. The light catches the glass vessel, creating
amber and golden highlights. The steam wisp is backlit slightly.

COLOR PALETTE: Amber gold of liquid wax (#D4A843), cream of cooled wax (#FAF3E8),
dark wood surface (#1A1A2E tones), warm skin tones, brass pitcher tones.

DEPTH OF FIELD: Very shallow. Sharp focus on the wax stream and the hands.
Soft bokeh on background. This creates intimacy and artisanal feeling.

MOOD: Precision, craftsmanship, care. The careful attention of a skilled maker.
A frame that makes the viewer understand this person knows exactly what she's doing.

TECHNICAL: Extreme macro-medium shot, hands fill approximately 60% of frame.
4:5 vertical. No text overlay needed — this image speaks for itself.

AVOID: Perfect manicured hands, professional studio lighting setups visible in frame,
any technology items, clean perfectly sterile surfaces. Imperfection is the point.
```

## PROMPT 6 — FONDO ATMOSFÉRICO OSCURO (Post 6: Quote aspiracional)

**Uso:** Fondo visual para superponerle una cita o texto inspiracional en Canva.
La imagen debe ser rica visualmente pero con suficiente "espacio" para el texto.

**Cuándo usar:** Post 6 (Lo que pasa cuando dejas de adivinar), quotes de marca,
momentos aspiracionales donde el texto es el protagonista

```
Generate a photorealistic atmospheric dark scene optimized as a background
for text overlay.

SCENE: Close-up view of a lit artisan candle on a dark wooden surface.
The scene is intentionally moody, cinematic, almost still-life fine art quality.

CANDLE: A round glass vessel with amber-tinted glass and cream-colored soy wax.
The flame is the only warm light source in the image — it creates dramatic
chiaroscuro lighting across the entire scene.

SURROUNDING ELEMENTS (minimal, intentional):
- The dark wooden surface with a slight natural grain texture
- One dried flower stem (eucalyptus or small rose) lying beside the candle, slightly wilted
- Soft, dark fabric draped in the far background
- The shadows are deep — the background is nearly #1A1A2E midnight dark

LIGHTING: The candle flame is the ONLY light source. Everything else is lit by its glow.
The center of the image (where the flame is) is warm amber #C17B2B.
The edges fade to deep midnight #1A1A2E.
This creates a natural vignette that is also the color palette of the brand.

KEY COMPOSITION REQUIREMENT: The candle occupies the bottom-left quarter of the image.
The upper two-thirds of the image is atmospheric dark space with just hints of shadow
and texture — this is specifically designed for large text overlay in Canva.
4:5 vertical format.

MOOD: Contemplative, intimate, emotional. The feeling just before a major decision.
The moment of stillness that comes from finally having clarity.

STYLE: Fine art photography, editorial quality, not commercial product photography.
Think: conceptual art book, not Amazon product listing.

AVOID: Multiple light sources, bright backgrounds, colorful elements, cluttered composition.
The power is in what is NOT shown. Restraint is the point.
```

## NOTAS DE USO Y MEJORA CONTINUA

### Cómo refinar un prompt cuando el resultado no es el esperado

**Si el color está mal:**
Añadir al final: `Strictly use this color palette: amber #C17B2B as dominant warm tone,
deep midnight #1A1A2E for shadows and background, cream #FAF3E8 for light surfaces.
No blue tones, no cold whites, no neon or saturated colors.`

**Si la iluminación es incorrecta:**
Añadir: `Lighting must be warm and directional, 3200-4000K color temperature,
simulating candlelight or morning window light. No harsh shadows, no studio flash,
no overhead fluorescent lighting.`

**Si parece stock photo genérico:**
Añadir: `This must look like documentary photography, not commercial stock photography.
Imperfections are intentional and valuable. The image should feel like it was caught
in a real moment, not staged for a photo shoot.`

**Si el personaje no parece real/local:**
Añadir: `The person must look specifically Colombian — warm brown skin tones,
dark features, dressed in natural artisan fabrics. No model-perfect styling.
Real hands that show evidence of craft work. Not a stock model.`
