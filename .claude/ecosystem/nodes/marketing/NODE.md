## Operational Rules

1. **Anchor image first** — upload `prompt-8.png` to every new Gemini/Nano Banana session before generating any image
2. **4:5 format mandatory** — all feed assets at 1080×1350px; all Reels at 9:16
3. **Publish schedule** — Tue/Thu 6–8pm Colombia time via Metricool; never post outside peak hours
4. **Save-over-likes** — optimize all content for saves and shares, not likes
5. **Max 5 hashtags** — hyper-specific niche hashtags only; no mass hashtag dumps
6. **SEO keyword parity** — every blog keyword must appear in at least one Instagram caption/Reel audio
7. **$0 budget** — zero paid advertising without explicit CEO approval + revenue justification
