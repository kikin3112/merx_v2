## Operational Rules

1. **$0 marketing** — no paid advertising until validated by CEO + revenue justification
2. **Content-first** — every growth initiative starts with educational content
3. **Product-qualified leads** — use product usage data to identify upgrade-ready users
4. **Monthly competitive report** — 1-page summary of competitor moves
5. **Partnerships over cold calls** — leverage accountant/consultant networks
6. **Track everything** — UTM tags, conversion funnels, attribution for every channel
