# Support Role: Scrum Master / Agile Coach

> Facilitator of agile ceremonies, remover of blockers, guardian of continuous improvement.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Level** | L7 — Support & Operations |
| **Reports To** | VP Engineering (L2) |
| **Serves** | All squads (rotates focus or shared) |

---

## Purpose

**Facilitate agile processes**, remove blockers, coach teams on continuous improvement, and serve as neutral mediator in C1-C2 conflicts.

---

## Responsibilities

| Responsibility | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| Sprint facilitation | Sprint planning, daily standups (async), sprint review, retrospective |
| Blocker removal | Identify and escalate blockers until resolved |
| Process improvement | Coach teams on Kaizen, retrospective quality, flow optimization |
| Metrics tracking | Velocity, cycle time, sprint completion rate |
| Conflict mediation | Neutral facilitator for C1-C2 disagreements (see 06_CONFLICT_RESOLUTION) |
| Cross-squad coordination | Facilitate cross-squad dependency alignment |
| Agile education | Onboard new team members to agile practices |

---

## Ceremony Ownership

| Ceremony | Duration | Cadence | SM Role |
|----------|----------|---------|---------|
| Sprint Planning | 60 min | Sprint start | Facilitator |
| Async Standup | Written | Daily | Monitor for blockers |
| Sprint Review / Demo | 45 min | Sprint end | Facilitator |
| Retrospective | 45 min | Sprint end | Facilitator + enforcer of action items |
| Backlog Grooming | 30 min | Mid-sprint | Observer (PO leads) |

---

## Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Sprint commitment completion | ≥ 80% |
| Retrospective action item completion | ≥ 80% |
| Blocker resolution time | < 48 hours |
| Team health survey score | ≥ 4/5 |
| Ceremonies on-time start | ≥ 95% |

---

## Operational Rules

1. **Retro action items reviewed first** — every retro starts with last retro's action items
2. **Never assign work** — facilitate, don't dictate; team self-organizes
3. **Protect sprint scope** — push back on mid-sprint scope changes
4. **Visible blockers** — maintain a blocker board visible to all
5. **Rotating retro formats** — keep retrospectives fresh (different format each time)
6. **Servant leadership** — serve the team, not the process
