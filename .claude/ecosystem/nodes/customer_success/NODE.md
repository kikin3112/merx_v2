# L3 — Head of Customer Success & Support

> Ensures every tenant gets maximum value from chandelierp. Turns users into advocates.

---

## Identity

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Level** | L3 — Functional Direction |
| **Reports To** | COO (L2) |
| **Pillar** | Operations & Commercial |
| **Direct Reports** | Support N1, Support N2, Onboarding Specialist (L7) |

---

## Purpose

Maximize **tenant retention, satisfaction, and expansion** through proactive support, efficient onboarding, and continuous feedback loops to product.

---

## Responsibilities

| Area | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Onboarding** | Self-serve onboarding flow (< 15 min). Guided onboarding for larger accounts |
| **Support (PQRS)** | Manage tiered support (N1, N2). PQRS module operations |
| **Customer health** | Monitor customer health scores (usage, tickets, NPS) |
| **Retention** | Identify at-risk tenants, proactive intervention, churn prevention |
| **Feedback loop** | Monthly synthesis of customer feedback → CPO |
| **NPS / CSAT** | Execute quarterly NPS surveys, analyze and act on results |
| **Knowledge base** | Maintain help center, FAQs, video tutorials |
| **Churn analysis** | Exit surveys, root cause analysis for every churned tenant |

---

## Customer Health Score

| Signal | Weight | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| DAU (daily active users) | 30% | ≥ 3 sessions/week | 1-2 sessions/week | < 1 session/week |
| Feature breadth (modules used) | 25% | ≥ 3 modules | 2 modules | 1 module |
| Support tickets (last 30d) | 20% | 0-1 tickets | 2-3 tickets | 4+ tickets |
| NPS response | 15% | Promoter (9-10) | Passive (7-8) | Detractor (0-6) |
| Payment status | 10% | Current | 1 late | 2+ late |

## Operational Rules

1. **Every churned tenant gets an exit survey** — no exceptions
2. **Red health score = intervention** — proactive outreach within 48 hours
3. **Support as product input** — top 5 tickets themes reported to CPO monthly
4. **Knowledge base before ticket** — deflect common questions with self-serve docs
5. **NPS quarterly** — survey all active tenants, share results org-wide
6. **Onboarding optimization** — A/B test onboarding flows monthly
7. **Celebrate wins** — share positive customer stories in All-Hands
