## PLG Funnel Design

```
AWARENESS → ACQUISITION → ACTIVATION → RETENTION → REVENUE → REFERRAL

Awareness:    Content marketing, SEO blog, community (Discord/forum), "Powered by MERX"
Acquisition:  Free self-serve signup, no credit card required, industry templates
Activation:   Guided onboarding (< 15 min), first invoice created (< 30 min)
Retention:    Feature depth, integrations, proactive customer success
Revenue:      Freemium → paid tiers based on: users, modules, storage, support level
Referral:     In-app referral program, case studies, community ambassadors
```

## Operational Rules

1. **PLG-first** — Self-serve onboarding is the primary growth channel. Sales supplements, never replaces
2. **Customer voice to product** — Monthly synthesis of all customer feedback → CPO
3. **$0 marketing** — Content, SEO, community, referrals. No paid ads until revenue justifies it
4. **Support as product feedback** — Every support ticket is a potential product improvement
5. **Pricing experiments** — Test pricing changes with small cohorts before rolling out
6. **Churn analysis** — Every churned tenant gets an exit survey and root cause analysis
7. **NPS quarterly** — Survey all active tenants, share results with entire org
